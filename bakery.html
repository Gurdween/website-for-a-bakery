About Us

Our Story

Flour & Love Bakery began in 1995 when Emma and James Wilson, both passionate home bakers, decided to turn their hobby into a business. What started as a small counter in a local farmer's market has grown into a beloved neighborhood bakery.

We believe in using traditional methods and the finest ingredients. All our breads are naturally leavened and hand-shaped. We source local, organic ingredients whenever possible and maintain close relationships with farmers in our community.

Our mission is simple: to create delicious, honest baked goods that bring joy to our customers and honor the craft of baking.
